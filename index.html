about

Engineer by curiosity.
Builder by nature.

I design and build systems that live at multiple layers of the stack — from bare-metal firmware on microcontrollers to full-stack applications, from custom PCB layouts to mathematical models and signal processing pipelines.

My work thrives on constraint. Hardware shapes software architecture. Analog imperfections become digital decisions. Elegant mathematics collapses complex control problems into something tractable.

When I'm not engineering, I'm reading about things I haven't built yet.
